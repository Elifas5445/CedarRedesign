Asheville Bed and Breakfast of Choice

The Cedar Crest Inn is a bed and breakfast in Asheville, NC, that, going on 29 years in business is the longest running Asheville B&B. Feel free to read our many positive TripAdvisor reviews - we offer a safe, clean, convenient and an overall fun place to be - and this definitely shows in our stellar TripAdvisor reviews.

Asheville, North Carolina has a lot to offer its' visitors. This city has incredible restaurants, a lifetime of outdoor activites, has been named Beer City USA 3 years running, and of course more arts and crafts than one could ever dream of. The great news our BnB boasts the best location to all there is to offer in this beautiful and vibrant city.

Our Asheville B&B is on a meticulously manicured 4 acre property located just three blocks from the entrance of the Biltmore Estate and the famous shopping and restaurants at Biltmore Village. Additionally, we are just one mile south of Downtown Asheville where restaurants, galleries, and shops are around every corner. We are just a mile east of the River Arts District which houses 125 + live art studios and four miles north of The Blue Ridge Parkway.

Our location is just one reason why the Cedar Crest Inn is the best Asheville bed and breakfast. We offer a wide variety of choices when it comes to our rooms. Regardless of where your needs or tastes lie, we have a room that will match style and budget. Whether you desire a traditonal B&B experience with victorian decor or a lavishly decorated modern and private room, Cedar Crest Inn has it all, with rooms as cheap as $99 per night!

The Cedar Crest Inn, offers many bed and breakfast amenities that hotels and resorts in Asheville just can't match: free wireless internet, parking, late check-out, Tazo teas, Seattle's Best Coffee, and desserts, endless wine and a three-course breakfast every day. No suprise charges here; everything is included in the price of your room. Don't forget about the many all-inclusive vacation packages we offer.
